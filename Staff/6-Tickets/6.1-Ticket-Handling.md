# 6.1 Ticket Handling

## 6.1.1 Purpose

This document defines the requirements and procedures for handling support tickets.

Tickets are used to organize member requests, reports, and issues requiring staff assistance.

---

## 6.1.2 Ticket Responsibilities

Staff members handling tickets must:

- Review the ticket information.
- Respond professionally.
- Provide accurate assistance.
- Follow applicable policies and procedures.

---

## 6.1.3 Ticket Response Standards

Staff members must:

- Respond within a reasonable timeframe.
- Communicate clearly.
- Request additional information when needed.
- Keep ticket discussions focused on the issue.

---

## 6.1.4 Ticket Conduct

Staff members must not:

- Ignore tickets without reason.
- Close tickets without proper review.
- Use tickets for personal conversations.
- Share confidential ticket information.

---

## 6.1.5 Ticket Documentation

Important ticket actions should be documented through approved systems.

Documentation may include:

- Issue summary.
- Actions taken.
- Final outcome.
