# 6.4 Closures

## 6.4.1 Purpose

This document defines the requirements for closing support tickets.

---

## 6.4.2 Closure Requirements

Before closing a ticket, staff members must ensure:

- The issue has been addressed.
- Required information has been collected.
- The member has received an appropriate response.

---

## 6.4.3 Improper Closures

Staff members must not close tickets:

- To avoid handling an issue.
- Without reviewing the situation.
- Due to personal disagreement.
- Without providing a reason when required.

---

## 6.4.4 Ticket Records

Important ticket outcomes should be recorded through approved systems.

Records should include:

- Issue handled.
- Actions taken.
- Final result.

---

## 6.4.5 Review

Management may review closed tickets to ensure proper procedures were followed.
