# 6.3 Response Times

## 6.3.1 Purpose

This document defines expectations for responding to tickets and member requests.

---

## 6.3.2 Response Requirements

Staff members assigned to tickets should respond within a reasonable timeframe based on availability and situation severity.

---

## 6.3.3 Priority Situations

Priority should be given to issues involving:

- Safety concerns.
- Security issues.
- Serious rule violations.
- Urgent member assistance.

---

## 6.3.4 Delayed Responses

If a staff member cannot continue handling a ticket, they should:

- Inform another available staff member.
- Transfer responsibility when necessary.
- Avoid leaving issues unresolved without notice.

---

## 6.3.5 Accountability

Repeated failure to respond to assigned tickets may result in activity review.
