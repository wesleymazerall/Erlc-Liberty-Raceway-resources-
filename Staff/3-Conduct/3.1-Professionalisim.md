# 3.1 Professionalism

## 3.1.1 Purpose

This document defines the professional standards required of all staff members.

Professional behavior is required to maintain trust, consistency, and a positive environment within the server.

---

## 3.1.2 Professional Conduct

All staff members must maintain professional conduct while performing staff duties.

Staff members must:

- Communicate respectfully with members and staff.
- Remain calm during disagreements or conflicts.
- Follow server policies and procedures.
- Make decisions based on facts and available information.
- Represent the server in a positive manner.

---

## 3.1.3 Unprofessional Conduct

The following behaviors are considered unprofessional:

- Insulting members or staff.
- Using hostile or aggressive language.
- Starting unnecessary arguments.
- Using staff authority to intimidate others.
- Acting in a manner that damages the reputation of the server.

Unprofessional behavior may result in disciplinary action.

---

## 3.1.4 Staff Representation

Staff members represent the server while performing staff duties.

Staff members are expected to understand that their actions may affect the reputation and trust of the Staff Team.

---

## 3.1.5 Accountability

Staff members are responsible for their actions and decisions.

Staff members must:

- Accept feedback from management.
- Correct mistakes when identified.
- Follow instructions from authorized staff members.

Failure to maintain professional standards may result in corrective action.
