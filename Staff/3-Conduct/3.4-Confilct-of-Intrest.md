# 3.4 Conflict of Interest

## 3.4.1 Purpose

This document defines conflicts of interest and establishes requirements for maintaining impartial staff decisions.

---

## 3.4.2 Definition

A conflict of interest occurs when a staff member's personal relationships, opinions, or interests could influence their ability to make a fair decision.

---

## 3.4.3 Examples

Conflicts of interest may include:

- Handling a report involving a close friend.
- Moderating a personal disagreement.
- Making decisions that personally benefit the staff member.
- Handling a situation involving personal bias.

---

## 3.4.4 Staff Requirements

Staff members must:

- Remain neutral.
- Avoid favoritism.
- Disclose conflicts when necessary.
- Request another staff member to handle the situation when impartiality cannot be maintained.

---

## 3.4.5 Prohibited Actions

Staff members must not:

- Use authority to benefit themselves.
- Punish users due to personal conflicts.
- Protect users because of personal relationships.

Failure to avoid conflicts of interest may result in disciplinary action.
