# 3.3 Confidentiality

## 3.3.1 Purpose

This document defines the requirements for protecting confidential staff information.

---

## 3.3.2 Confidential Information

Confidential information includes:

- Staff discussions.
- Internal reports.
- Moderation records.
- Investigation details.
- Staff decisions.
- Private documentation.

---

## 3.3.3 Access Restrictions

Staff members may access confidential information only for authorized staff responsibilities.

Access does not provide permission to share or distribute information.

---

## 3.3.4 Information Disclosure

Staff members must not:

- Share staff-only conversations.
- Leak screenshots from private channels.
- Share reports or logs.
- Provide internal information to unauthorized users.

---

## 3.3.5 Confidentiality Violations

Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information is a serious policy violation.

Violations may result in:

- Removal of permissions.
- Disciplinary action.
- Removal from the Staff Team.
