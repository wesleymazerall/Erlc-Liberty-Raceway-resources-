# 3.2 Respect

## 3.2.1 Purpose

This document defines the standards of respect required between staff members and the community.

---

## 3.2.2 Respect Requirements

All staff members must treat members and staff with fairness and respect.

Staff members must not allow:

- Personal opinions.
- Friendships.
- Disagreements.
- Personal conflicts.

to influence how they treat others.

---

## 3.2.3 Prohibited Behavior

Staff members must not:

- Harass others.
- Bully others.
- Discriminate against others.
- Target individuals unfairly.
- Retaliate against users or staff.
- Abuse their position.

---

## 3.2.4 Conflict Handling

Disagreements must be handled privately and professionally.

Staff members must not create public conflicts or arguments that negatively affect the server.

---

## 3.2.5 Fair Treatment

All members must receive equal treatment under server policies.

Staff members must enforce rules consistently regardless of personal relationships or opinions.
