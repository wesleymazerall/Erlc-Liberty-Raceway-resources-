# 4.2 Timeouts

## 4.2.1 Purpose

This document defines the requirements for issuing member timeouts.

Timeouts are used to temporarily restrict communication when a member's behavior requires additional action.

---

## 4.2.2 When to Issue a Timeout

A timeout may be issued when a member:

- Continues breaking rules after warnings.
- Spams or disrupts conversations.
- Creates unnecessary conflict.
- Engages in behavior requiring temporary restriction.

---

## 4.2.3 Timeout Requirements

Staff members must:

- Use appropriate timeout durations.
- Provide a valid reason.
- Follow moderation procedures.

Staff members must not use timeouts:

- To punish personal disagreements.
- As a joke.
- Without a valid reason.

---

## 4.2.4 Documentation

Timeout actions should include:

- Member affected.
- Reason.
- Duration.
- Staff member responsible.

---

## 4.2.5 Review

Higher-ranking staff may review timeout decisions to ensure proper use of moderation authority.
