# 4.5 Evidence

## 4.5.1 Purpose

This document defines how evidence must be collected, reviewed, and used during moderation actions.

---

## 4.5.2 Acceptable Evidence

Evidence may include:

- Screenshots.
- Message history.
- Server logs.
- Recordings.
- Reports from members or staff.

---

## 4.5.3 Evidence Standards

Evidence must be:

- Relevant to the situation.
- Accurate.
- Related to the reported behavior.

Staff members must not intentionally ignore relevant evidence.

---

## 4.5.4 Evidence Handling

Evidence must be stored and shared only through approved systems.

Confidential evidence must not be shared with unauthorized users.

---

## 4.5.5 False Evidence

Providing false, edited, or misleading evidence is prohibited.

Attempting to manipulate moderation decisions through false information may result in disciplinary action.
