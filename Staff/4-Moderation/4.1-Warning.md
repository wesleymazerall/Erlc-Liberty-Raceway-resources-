# 4.1 Warnings

## 4.1.1 Purpose

This document defines the requirements and procedures for issuing warnings to members.

Warnings are used to notify members of rule violations and provide an opportunity for correction before further action is taken.

---

## 4.1.2 When to Issue a Warning

Staff members should issue warnings when a member:

- Commits a minor rule violation.
- Shows behavior that requires correction.
- Violates a rule that does not require immediate punishment.

---

## 4.1.3 Warning Requirements

Warnings must:

- Clearly identify the rule violation.
- Explain what behavior must change.
- Remain professional and respectful.
- Be appropriate for the situation.

Staff members must not issue warnings:

- As a joke.
- To target a user.
- Due to personal disagreements.

---

## 4.1.4 Documentation

Warnings may be recorded through approved moderation systems.

Records should include:

- User involved.
- Reason for warning.
- Date and time.
- Staff member issuing the warning.

---

## 4.1.5 Repeated Violations

Repeated rule violations after warnings may result in stronger moderation actions.
