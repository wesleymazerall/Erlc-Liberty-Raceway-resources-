# 4.4 Bans

## 4.4.1 Purpose

This document defines the requirements for issuing permanent or extended removals from the server.

Bans are reserved for serious violations or situations where continued access creates a risk to the community.

---

## 4.4.2 When to Issue a Ban

A ban may be issued for:

- Severe rule violations.
- Repeated violations after previous actions.
- Threats or serious harmful behavior.
- Actions that damage server safety or security.

---

## 4.4.3 Ban Requirements

Staff members must:

- Have a valid reason.
- Review available evidence.
- Follow the approval process if required.

---

## 4.4.4 Documentation

Ban records must include:

- Member banned.
- Reason.
- Evidence when available.
- Staff member responsible.
- Date and time.

---

## 4.4.5 Ban Review

Management may review bans to ensure actions were appropriate and consistent with policy.
