# 4.3 Kicks

## 4.3.1 Purpose

This document defines the requirements for removing members from the server through a kick action.

A kick is a temporary removal used when a member's continued presence requires intervention but does not require a permanent ban.

---

## 4.3.2 When to Issue a Kick

A kick may be issued when a member:

- Repeatedly violates rules.
- Disrupts server operations.
- Refuses to follow staff instructions.
- Creates significant issues requiring removal.

---

## 4.3.3 Kick Requirements

Before issuing a kick, staff members must:

- Confirm the reason is valid.
- Review available information.
- Ensure the action follows server policy.

---

## 4.3.4 Documentation

Kick records should include:

- Member removed.
- Reason for removal.
- Date and time.
- Staff member responsible.

---

## 4.3.5 Abuse Prevention

Staff members must not use kicks:

- To remove users they dislike.
- To avoid resolving conflicts.
- Without proper justification.
