# 5.3 Audit Logs

## 5.3.1 Purpose

This document defines the purpose and handling of audit logs related to staff actions.

---

## 5.3.2 Audit Log Purpose

Audit logs are used to:

- Track staff actions.
- Review moderation decisions.
- Investigate issues.
- Maintain accountability.

---

## 5.3.3 Staff Responsibility

Staff members must understand that actions performed with staff permissions may be reviewed.

Staff members are responsible for actions taken through their accounts.

---

## 5.3.4 Log Integrity

Staff members must not:

- Delete logs.
- Hide actions.
- Manipulate records.
- Attempt to avoid tracking.

---

## 5.3.5 Review Authority

Authorized management may review audit logs at any time for operational or disciplinary purposes.
