# 5.1 Permission Usage

## 5.1.1 Purpose

This document defines the proper use of staff permissions and administrative tools.

Staff permissions exist only to allow staff members to complete assigned responsibilities.

---

## 5.1.2 Authorized Use

Staff members may use permissions only for:

- Moderation actions.
- Staff responsibilities.
- Server management tasks.
- Assisting members.

---

## 5.1.3 Unauthorized Use

Staff members must not use permissions:

- For personal benefit.
- To target users.
- To bypass rules.
- As a joke or for entertainment.
- To assist friends unfairly.

---

## 5.1.4 Permission Responsibility

Staff members are responsible for every action performed using their permissions.

Sharing accounts, credentials, or access to staff tools is prohibited.

---

## 5.1.5 Violations

Improper use of permissions may result in:

- Removal of permissions.
- Disciplinary action.
- Removal from the Staff Team.
