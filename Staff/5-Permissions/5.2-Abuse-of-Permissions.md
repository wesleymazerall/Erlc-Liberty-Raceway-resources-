# 5.2 Abuse of Permissions

## 5.2.1 Purpose

This document defines what is considered abuse of staff permissions.

---

## 5.2.2 Definition

Abuse of permissions occurs when a staff member uses their access or authority in a way that is unauthorized, unfair, or harmful.

---

## 5.2.3 Examples of Abuse

Examples include:

- Punishing users without valid reasons.
- Changing server settings without authorization.
- Using commands for personal entertainment.
- Overriding another staff member without authority.
- Using permissions to avoid consequences.

---

## 5.2.4 Prevention

Staff members must:

- Understand their available permissions.
- Use permissions only when necessary.
- Ask for approval when unsure.

---

## 5.2.5 Consequences

Permission abuse is considered a serious violation.

Possible consequences include:

- Permission removal.
- Demotion.
- Removal from staff.
