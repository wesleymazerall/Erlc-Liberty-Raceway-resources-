# 8.3 Templates

## 8.3.1 Purpose

This document defines the use of official templates for staff communication and documentation.

Templates exist to provide consistency and ensure important information is recorded correctly.

---

## 8.3.2 Template Usage

Staff members should use approved templates when completing tasks such as:

- Reports.
- Staff documentation.
- Moderation records.
- Internal requests.

---

## 8.3.3 Template Modification

Staff members must not modify official templates in a way that removes required information.

---

## 8.3.4 Template Responsibility

Staff members are responsible for ensuring submitted information is accurate and complete.

Incorrect or incomplete documentation may require correction.
