# 8.2 Useful Links

## 8.2.1 Purpose

This document contains approved resources and links used by the Staff Team.

---

## 8.2.2 Approved Resources

Staff members should use only approved resources when performing staff duties.

Resources may include:

- Staff tools.
- Documentation.
- Reporting systems.
- Management resources.

---

## 8.2.3 Link Management

Only authorized staff members may add, remove, or modify official staff resources.

---

## 8.2.4 External Resources

External resources must be reviewed before being used for official staff operations.
