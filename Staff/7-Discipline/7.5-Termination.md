# 7.5 Termination

## 7.5.1 Purpose

This document defines the process for removing a staff member from the Staff Team.

---

## 7.5.2 Reasons for Removal

A staff member may be removed due to:

- Serious policy violations.
- Abuse of authority.
- Repeated failure to meet expectations.
- Security concerns.
- Loss of trust.

---

## 7.5.3 Removal Authority

Staff removal decisions may only be made by authorized management.

---

## 7.5.4 Removal Process

When removed, a staff member may lose:

- Staff roles.
- Permissions.
- Access to staff resources.

---

## 7.5.5 Final Decision

Management decisions regarding staff removal are final unless an approved review process exists.
