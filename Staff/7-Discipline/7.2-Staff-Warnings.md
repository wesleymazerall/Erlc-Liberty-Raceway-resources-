# 7.2 Staff Warnings

## 7.2.1 Purpose

This document defines formal warnings issued to staff members for policy violations or performance issues.

---

## 7.2.2 Reasons for a Staff Warning

A staff warning may be issued for:

- Breaking staff policies.
- Misusing permissions.
- Failing responsibilities.
- Repeated performance issues.
- Unprofessional behavior.

---

## 7.2.3 Warning Process

A staff warning should include:

- The issue being addressed.
- Relevant policy or expectation.
- Required improvement.
- Possible consequences of future violations.

---

## 7.2.4 Staff Responsibility

A staff member receiving a warning is expected to:

- Understand the issue.
- Correct the behavior.
- Follow future requirements.

---

## 7.2.5 Further Violations

Additional violations may result in stronger disciplinary action.
