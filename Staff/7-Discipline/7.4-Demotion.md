# 7.4 Demotion

## 7.4.1 Purpose

This document defines when a staff member may have their rank or responsibilities reduced.

---

## 7.4.2 Reasons for Demotion

A staff member may be demoted due to:

- Failure to meet responsibilities.
- Abuse of authority.
- Repeated policy violations.
- Inability to perform assigned duties.

---

## 7.4.3 Demotion Authority

Only authorized management may approve staff demotions.

---

## 7.4.4 After Demotion

A demoted staff member:

- Loses responsibilities associated with the previous rank.
- Must follow the requirements of their new position.
- May be reviewed for future advancement.

---

## 7.4.5 Accountability

Demotion decisions are based on staff performance, conduct, and server needs.
