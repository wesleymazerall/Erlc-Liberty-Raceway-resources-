# 7.3 Probation

## 7.3.1 Purpose

This document defines the use of staff probation as a corrective action.

Probation allows a staff member to continue their position while being monitored for improvement.

---

## 7.3.2 Reasons for Probation

A staff member may be placed on probation due to:

- Repeated policy violations.
- Performance concerns.
- Activity issues.
- Failure to meet staff expectations.

---

## 7.3.3 Probation Requirements

During probation, a staff member may be required to:

- Follow additional expectations.
- Receive increased review.
- Demonstrate improvement.
- Complete assigned corrective actions.

---

## 7.3.4 Probation Violations

Failure to improve during probation may result in:

- Demotion.
- Removal of permissions.
- Removal from staff.

---

## 7.3.5 Completion

Successful completion of probation does not remove previous accountability records.
