# 2.4 Activity Reviews

## 2.4.1 Purpose

This document defines the process used by management to review staff activity, performance, and continued eligibility for a Staff Team position.

Activity reviews ensure that all staff members are actively contributing and maintaining the standards required for their role.

---

# 2.4.2 Review Authority

Management has the authority to conduct staff activity reviews at any time.

Staff members may be reviewed:

- During scheduled reviews.
- After reports of inactivity.
- After extended absences.
- When performance concerns are identified.

Staff members are not entitled to prior notice before a review is conducted.

---

# 2.4.3 Review Criteria

Activity reviews may evaluate:

- Server participation.
- Completion of assigned responsibilities.
- Response to staff communications.
- Moderation activity.
- Ticket/support activity.
- Compliance with staff policies.
- Professional conduct.
- Attendance history.

---

# 2.4.4 Performance Standards

Staff members are expected to consistently meet the requirements of their position.

A staff member may be considered below standard if they:

- Frequently fail to complete assigned duties.
- Ignore required communications.
- Require repeated reminders to complete responsibilities.
- Demonstrate inconsistent participation.

---

# 2.4.5 Review Outcomes

After an activity review, management may determine one of the following outcomes:

## Approved

The staff member meets expectations and continues normal duties.

## Improvement Required

The staff member is required to improve specific areas of performance.

Additional monitoring may be applied.

## Corrective Action Required

The staff member may receive disciplinary action due to failure to meet requirements.

## Removal Review

The staff member may be considered for removal from the Staff Team.

---

# 2.4.6 Staff Response

If a staff member receives feedback from an activity review, they are expected to:

- Acknowledge the feedback.
- Address identified issues.
- Follow any improvement requirements.

Refusal to cooperate with a review may result in further action.

---

# 2.4.7 Documentation

Management may document activity reviews for internal staff records.

Documentation may include:

- Review date.
- Staff member reviewed.
- Findings.
- Required actions.
- Final decision.

---

# 2.4.8 Final Authority

Management has final authority over activity review decisions.

Activity reviews are conducted to maintain staff reliability, organization, and operational standards.
