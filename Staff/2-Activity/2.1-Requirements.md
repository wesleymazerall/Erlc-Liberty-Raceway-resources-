# 2.1 Activity Requirements

## 2.1.1 Purpose

This document defines the minimum activity expectations required for all Staff Team members.

Staff positions require active participation. Staff members who fail to meet activity requirements may be subject to review or removal.

---

# 2.1.2 Activity Standard

All staff members are required to maintain consistent activity within the server and assigned staff responsibilities.

Activity includes:

- Monitoring assigned areas.
- Assisting members.
- Participating in staff discussions.
- Completing assigned tasks.
- Responding to staff-related requests.

Simply remaining in the server without participating does not qualify as active staff participation.

---

# 2.1.3 Availability Requirements

Staff members must remain reasonably available based on their assigned position and responsibilities.

Staff members are expected to:

- Check the server regularly.
- Review important staff communications.
- Respond when assistance is required.
- Complete assigned responsibilities within a reasonable timeframe.

---

# 2.1.4 Inactivity

A staff member is considered inactive when they fail to participate in staff duties for an extended period without approval or communication.

Examples of inactivity include:

- Not responding to staff messages.
- Not performing assigned duties.
- Not participating in required staff activities.
- Being absent without notification.

---

# 2.1.5 Activity Expectations by Rank

Activity requirements may vary depending on staff rank and responsibility.

Higher-ranking staff members may have additional activity expectations due to increased responsibilities.

Staff members must meet the requirements assigned to their position.

---

# 2.1.6 Communication During Absence

If a staff member is unable to meet activity requirements, they are required to submit a Leave of Absence (LOA) request before becoming inactive.

All LOA requests must be submitted through the **Melonly Moderator Panel** using the designated LOA request system.

A staff member must not rely only on verbal notice, direct messages, or informal communication as an official absence request.

The LOA request must include:

- Expected start date of absence.
- Expected return date.
- Reason for absence (optional if private).
- Any responsibilities that require coverage.

A staff member's absence is not considered approved until the LOA request has been reviewed and accepted by authorized staff.

Failure to submit an LOA request before an extended absence may result in the absence being classified as unapproved inactivity.

Unapproved inactivity may result in disciplinary action, including removal from the Staff Team.
---

# 2.1.7 Activity Reviews

Management may review staff activity at any time.

Reviews may consider:

- Participation history.
- Completion of responsibilities.
- Communication.
- Overall contribution.

Activity reviews are used to ensure staff positions are being properly maintained.

---

# 2.1.8 Failure to Meet Requirements

Failure to meet activity requirements may result in:

- Reminder or warning.
- Temporary removal of responsibilities.
- Rank reduction.
- Removal from the Staff Team.

Management determines appropriate action based on the circumstances.
