# 1.1 Purpose

## 1.1.1 Document Purpose

This document defines the purpose, authority, and expectations of the Staff Team.

All staff members are required to understand and follow the policies outlined within this documentation.

Failure to follow these requirements may result in disciplinary action, including removal from the Staff Team.

---

## 1.1.2 Staff Team Purpose

The purpose of the Staff Team is to maintain the operation, safety, and organization of the server.

Staff members are responsible for:

- Enforcing server policies.
- Assisting community members.
- Handling moderation situations.
- Maintaining a professional environment.
- Protecting the integrity of the server.

---

## 1.1.3 Staff Authority

Staff authority is provided only for the purpose of completing assigned staff responsibilities.

Staff members are authorized to use staff permissions only when:

- Performing moderation duties.
- Assisting members.
- Managing server-related issues.
- Completing assigned tasks.

Staff permissions must not be used for personal benefit, entertainment, retaliation, or any action unrelated to staff responsibilities.

---

## 1.1.4 Staff Responsibility

By accepting a staff position, the staff member agrees to:

1. Follow all server rules and staff policies.
2. Maintain professional behavior at all times.
3. Use staff permissions responsibly.
4. Treat all members fairly and without bias.
5. Follow instructions provided by higher-ranking staff members.

---

## 1.1.5 Policy Enforcement

All staff members are subject to review by server management.

Management reserves the right to:

- Review staff actions.
- Request explanations for decisions.
- Correct improper actions.
- Remove staff permissions.
- Remove staff members who fail to meet expectations.

---

## 1.1.6 Scope

This document applies to all staff members regardless of rank or position.

No staff member is exempt from these policies.

Holding a higher staff rank does not remove the requirement to follow this handbook.
