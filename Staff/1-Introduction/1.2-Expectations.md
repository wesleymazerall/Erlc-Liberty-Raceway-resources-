# 1.2 Expectations

## 1.2.1 Purpose

This document defines the minimum standards and expectations required of all Staff Team members.

All staff members are required to meet these expectations while holding a staff position.

Failure to meet these expectations may result in corrective action or removal from the Staff Team.

---

## 1.2.2 Professional Conduct

All staff members must maintain professional conduct at all times.

Staff members must:

- Communicate respectfully with all members and staff.
- Remain calm during disagreements or conflicts.
- Make decisions based on server policies and available information.
- Represent the server in a positive manner.

Staff members must not:

- Engage in unnecessary arguments.
- Use their position to intimidate or threaten members.
- Treat members unfairly due to personal opinions.
- Create conflict within the community.

---

## 1.2.3 Communication Requirements

Staff members are required to communicate clearly and appropriately.

Staff members must:

- Use the correct channels for staff discussions.
- Provide relevant information when reporting issues.
- Respond to staff requests when available.
- Ask for clarification when unsure about a procedure.

Staff members must not:

- Ignore important staff communications without reason.
- Share internal staff discussions with non-staff members.
- Provide false or misleading information.

---

## 1.2.4 Decision Making

Staff members are expected to make decisions based on:

- Server rules.
- Staff procedures.
- Available evidence.
- Guidance from higher-ranking staff.

Staff members must not make decisions based on:

- Personal friendships.
- Personal disagreements.
- Favoritism.
- Outside conflicts.

---

## 1.2.5 Staff Activity

All staff members are required to maintain reasonable activity.

Staff members must:

- Participate in server operations.
- Monitor assigned responsibilities.
- Remain available when scheduled or assigned.

Failure to maintain activity without an approved absence may result in review of the staff member's position.

---

## 1.2.6 Accountability

All staff members are responsible for their actions while performing staff duties.

Staff members must:

- Accept feedback from management.
- Correct mistakes when identified.
- Follow instructions from authorized staff members.

Attempting to avoid responsibility, hide mistakes, or refuse reasonable staff direction may result in disciplinary action.

---

## 1.2.7 Staff Standard

Staff members are representatives of the server.

Every staff member is expected to maintain the standards defined in this handbook at all times while holding a staff position.
